Securing a Flowforming Machine

Project conducted with 3 classmates during a whole school year, supervised by teachers and electrical maintenance technicians.

About the project

On my campus at Arts et MÃ©tiers in Cluny there was an old Flowforming Machine that was barely used because it was too old to respect the current safety standards. With a team of 3 classmates, our project was to dismantle the old electronic circuits, study them (because all the documentation had disappeared) and rebuild a working and safe electronic system.

We learned a lot of skills and knowledge regarding industrial machinery, maintenance practices and safety standards.

I'm happy to have finished this project because thanks to us they now use the flow forming machine again during school pratical exercises.
